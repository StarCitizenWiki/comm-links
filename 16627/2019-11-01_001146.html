THE ANVIL HURRICANE

PACK A WALLOP in Alpha 3.2

Anvil Aerospace delivers a focused fighter that packs a deadly punch into a slight fuselage.

The Hurricane compensates for its lack of creature comforts with powerful armament - its six guns are capable of blasting through nearly anything. Hurricane pilots have yet to find an enemy shield they can't knock down.

Such a heavy concentration of ordnance requires a dedicated gunner, so find a buddy with an itchy trigger finger, engage maximum firepower, and blow your enemies halfway across the galaxy.

Commander Jackson Russell, UEE Navy (Ret.)

"If you're looking to blast a target out of the solar system, the Hurricane gets the job done."

THE ORIGIN 600i EXPLORER

REDEFINE EXPERIENCE in Alpha 3.2

Origin Jumpworks offer long-range luxury with the opulent 600i.

This multi-role luxury vessel features an exquisitely detailed hull that balances performance and versatility in a sleek and timeless form. Explore the far reaches of the universe without sacrificing style or comfort.

Redefine luxury and explore the unknown in your 600i.

Explore the universe and a new standard in design, class, and sophistication that's still built to handle the unexpected.

Adventure Starts Here

A symphony in motion

THE AEGIS ECLIPSE

SHADOWS FALL in Alpha 3.2

Aegis Dynamics drop a bomber designed to get in and strike before it's spotted.

The Aegis Eclipse brings high tech covert offensive and defensive capabilities, long trusted in UEE service, to the civilian market with explosive results.

Stay off the radar in your Eclipse.

The UEE's best but least known protector, declassified and available to you.

Stealth in the Shadows

Be Heard, Not Seen

THE VANDUUL BLADE

RAVAGE in Alpha 3.2

Esperia painstakingly recreates the vicious Vanduul fighter for the civilian human market.

The Vanduul use these speedy skirmishers to perform quick, precise strikes, and chase down fleeing enemies. Now, thanks to Esperia, you too can harness the nimble brutality of the Vanduul fleet.

Harness the wrath of the Vanduul in your Blade.

The speed, power, and brutality of the Vanduul fleet at your fingertips.

Enemy Mine

Behold the Blade

Want to know more? Visit the complete Blade ship page.

THE AEGIS AVENGER

WATCH THE SKIES in Alpha 3.2

Aegis Dynamics updates their dependable Avenger series for 2948.

The Stalker remains a model of vigilance, the Titan stands as the perfect small freighter for keeping loads light and well protected, and the Warlock brings non-lethal enemy suppression to the table with its EMP module.

Aegis has also applied this design overhaul to their special Renegade commemorative edition, making it available again for a limited time.

Stay vigilant in your Avenger.

The Avenger series has a long and storied history in patrol service with the UEE Advocacy.

Reliability and Versatility
