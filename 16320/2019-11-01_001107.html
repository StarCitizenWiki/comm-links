End of Year Subscriber Survey


End of Year Subscriber Survey

Let Us Know What You Think

Imperators and Centurions,

We’re looking ahead to 2018. You may have seen the Subscriber’s Program Feedback Discussion thread posted in the Den last week. We appreciate all the feedback we’ve received so far, and we encourage you to participate if you haven’t already. With the polls below, we’re look for some more insight on how you feel about the Subscription Program Perks, Jump Point and the shows brought to the community by Subscribers. We’ll follow up with additional information in the coming weeks.

Let’s get started with some questions about the Perks themselves.

2017 Subscriber Perks

Hangar Flair
Subscribers receive exclusive digital flair items every month, like wall art or collectibles to display around your hangar.

Total Votes: 6998

Ship of the Month
Subscribers get the chance to test-fly a different ship every month.

Total Votes: 6998

Subscriber's Den
Forum and chat room access exclusive to subscribers.

Total Votes: 6998

The Vault
A new album of concept, work in progress and behind the scenes images that’s posted once a week.

Total Votes: 6998

Early Access to Purchase Event Tickets
Subscribers get a head-start on the opportunity to purchase tickets for upcoming CIG events.

Total Votes: 6998

Coupons
Once you accumulate 12 months of subscription time, subscribers are eligible for a coupon that can be used on a future digital purchase. Centurions get a 10% discount ($50 cap) and Imperators get a 20% discount ($100 cap.)

Total Votes: 6998

Subscriber's Town Hall
A monthly show featuring a revolving roundtable of CIG developers where only subscribers can ask the questions.

Total Votes: 6998

Discounted Annual Plans & Passes
If you purchase an annual Centurion or Imperator plan, you’ll get 12 months of subscription time for the price of 11 months. (Please note this applies only to One Year Annual plans/passes, and does not apply to monthly plans/passes.)

Total Votes: 6998

Merchandise Discounts & Exclusive Digital Sales
Subscribers get access to discounted and exclusive physical merchandise, as well as limited digital ship sales.

Total Votes: 6998

Test Pilot/REC Access
A monthly allotment of REC for use in the Electronic Access store. Centurions receive 20,000 REC monthly and Imperators receive 40,000 REC monthly.

Total Votes: 6998

Imperator Test Flight
Imperator-level subscribers have access to test fly all newly flight-ready ships and variants when patches go live, for the duration of one week.

Total Votes: 6998

Access to Jump Point
Subscribers get access to Jump Point, Star Citizen’s official digital magazine.

Total Votes: 6998

What’s Your Favorite Part of Jump Point?
Pick between the five sections in every issue of Jump Point magazine.

Total Votes: 6998

BONUS QUESTION: What is your favorite non-ATV broadcast?

Total Votes: 6119
Vote!
Broadcasts

Star Citizen produces and releases between 15 and 20 videos each month, funded through the generous contributions of Subscribers. In the list above, please pick your favorite Non-Around the Verse broadcast currently in production.

End Transmission
