Roadmap Roundup - September 27th, 2019

Roadmap Roundup

Hi everyone,

Each and every week, we accompany the Roadmap update with a brief explanatory note to give you insight into the decision-making that led to any changes. This is part of an effort to make our communications more transparent, more specific, and more insightful for all of you who help to make Star Citizen and Squadron 42 possible.

With that, let’s dive into this week’s roundup!

-CIG Community Team

Notable Changes for September 27th, 2019

The Banu Defender, Caves, and Ship Rentals have gone into polishing in preparation for their launch in Star Citizen Alpha 3.7.

RSI Mantis

A card has been added to the Roadmap for the recently revealed RSI Mantis, which will hit the live servers during the 3.7 patch cycle.

Quantum Enforcement v1

A card has been added to the Roadmap for the recently revealed Interdiction gameplay, which will hit the live servers during the 3.7 patch cycle.

MacFlex “Rucksack” Core

A card has been added to the Roadmap for the MacFlex “Rucksack” Core armor torso piece, which will hit the live servers in 3.7.

Weapons

The weapons team recently re-prioritized and shifted their weapons schedule around resulting in several changes:

  • The Behring P6-LR Ballistic Sniper Rifle has moved up from 3.9 to 3.8.
  • The Klaus & Werner Sledge Mass Driver Revision has moved back from 3.8 to 3.9.
  • The Behring GP-33 Grenade Launcher has moved from 3.8 to 4.0.
  • The Verified Offworld Laser Technologies Parallax Assault Rifle has been temporarily removed from the Roadmap while discussions continue about where it’s most likely to land.

That’s all for this week’s Roundup – join the discussion on Spectrum

End Transmission
