Wingman's Hangar - Episode 26

It’s time for another episode of Wingman’s Hangar! Tune in today at 11 AM CST (-5 GMT) for the stream, or catch the HD version afterwards. However you watch it, you don’t want to miss it: you’ll meet Community Manager Ben Lesnick and hear the latest about Star Citizen’s development. Then be sure and check back later today for the 300i reveal and promotion! We’re very excited to share some of the great details we’ve worked out for this one.
