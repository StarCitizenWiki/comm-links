Holiday Promotion 2019

Happy Holidays!

Spread some holiday cheer!


Take advantage of deals so sweet, they’re out of this world – Our seasonal starter pack is the perfect way to treat yourself or pick up gifts for friends and family yearning to explore the stars.



You can also get into the spirit of the season (and maybe even win a fabulous prize) by entering our two holiday-themed contests. Check out all the details about those below.

HOLIDAY SCREENSHOT CONTEST

2018 Holiday Screenshot by Terada

Celebrate the holidays with us in the ‘verse!


Show us how you celebrate in the ‘verse by entering our 2019 Screenshot Contest. Set up a seasonal scene and snap a pic of you and your friends celebrating a very Star Citizen holiday. Spruce up your scene with a handful of exciting in-game holiday items in Alpha 3.8 (check them all out now on the PTU)!



Snap a sensational screenshot and be entered into the running for one of these fabulous prizes:

  • 1st Place: ARGO MOLE in-game ship with LTI
  • 2nd Place: RSI Mantis in-game ship with LTI
  • 3rd Place: AEGIS Avenger Titan in-game ship with LTI

What are you waiting for? All the details can be found on Spectrum here.

HOLIDAY GREETINGS CARD CONTEST

2018 Holiday Card by RON1N-Kenshin

Season’s greetings from the ‘verse!


Let your creativity flow and get into the spirit of the season with our Holiday Greetings Card Contest. Fire up an image-editing program or grab some paper and create a Star Citizen (or Squadron 42) themed holiday greetings card.



Deliver some holiday cheer with prizes for our top 3 submissions:

  • 1st Place: Drake Caterpillar game package with LTI
  • 2nd Place: MISC Freelancer MAX game package with LTI
  • 3rd Place: Drake Cutlass Black game package with LTI


Grab all the details and submit your creation on Spectrum here.

UNDER THE TREE

Holiday Spoils!


Some of the major hubs throughout the Stanton system are getting festive, with hidden gifts popping up with the introduction of Alpha 3.8 (check out the festivities now on the PTU!). Snatch up any presents you come across and use them to turn your ship into a winter wonderland, or sell them at your local commodity trading kiosk for profit!

The Season for Giving

Gifts for your friends and family, all around the ‘verse!


Give the gift of Star Citizen to your loved ones this year with these ships, available to acquire for yourself or to send as gifts to the prospective pilots in your life.



Get them while they last! These deals will melt away with the holiday season’s end.
